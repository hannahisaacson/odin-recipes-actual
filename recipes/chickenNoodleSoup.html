Chicken Noodle Soup

A great option for dinner! It might take a while to cook, but it's totally worth all the hard work!

Please take the time to look below and see how to create this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Celerye
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Broth
  • Garlic
  • Parsely
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Cut veggies and cook in pan.
  2. Put chicken in and pour broth.
  3. Bring to a boil and cook for 30 minutes.
  4. Put noodles in.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsely.
