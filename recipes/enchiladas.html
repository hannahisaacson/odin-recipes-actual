Enchiladas

A great option for dinner! It might take a while to cook, but it's totally worth all the hard work!

Please take the time to look below and see how to create this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef.
  2. Roll beef in tortilla.
  3. Lay the rolled tortillas in a dish.
  4. Cover with sauce.
  5. Put in the oven and cook for an hour at 350 degrees F.
